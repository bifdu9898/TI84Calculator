Supported Functions

This online emulator supports all standard TI-84 Plus CE functions including:

  • Trigonometric functions (sin, cos, tan, and inverses)

  • Matrix operations (up to 10x10 matrices)
  • Complex number calculations
  • Statistical calculations and distributions

  • List operations
